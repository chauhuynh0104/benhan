Asthma – A Common Global Disease

Asthma is a chronic disease that affects the respiratory system. People with asthma often experience symptoms such as coughing, wheezing, chest tightness, and difficulty breathing. These symptoms happen because the airways become narrow and swollen, making it hard for air to move in and out of the lungs.

Asthma attacks are often triggered by allergens and environmental factors. Common triggers include dust, pollen, pet hair, air pollution, strong chemicals, and tobacco smoke. In some people, physical exercise or cold air can also cause asthma symptoms to become worse.

Asthma is one of the most common chronic diseases among children, but adults can suffer from it as well. Although asthma cannot be completely cured, it can usually be controlled with proper treatment. Many patients use inhalers and take regular medication to reduce inflammation in the airways and prevent asthma attacks.

According to health experts, asthma is a major public health problem worldwide. A large number of asthma-related deaths occur in low and middle-income countries due to limited access to medical care. With correct treatment, education, and avoidance of triggers, most people with asthma can live a normal and active life.

The Food Pyramid and a Balanced Diet

Good nutrition plays an important role in maintaining good health and preventing disease. One of the most common guides for healthy eating is the food pyramid. It shows different groups of food and how much of each group people should eat every day.

At the bottom of the pyramid are foods such as rice, bread, pasta, cereal, and potatoes. These foods provide the body with energy and should be eaten in the largest amount. The next level includes fruit and vegetables, which are rich in vitamins, minerals, and fibre. Eating enough fruit and vegetables helps to protect the body from illness.

The third level of the pyramid consists of meat, fish, eggs, beans, and dairy products. These foods are important sources of protein and calcium, which help the body grow and repair itself. However, they should be eaten in moderate amounts.

At the top of the pyramid are fats, oils, and sweets. These foods contain a lot of energy but very few nutrients. Eating too much of them can lead to overweight and other health problems such as heart disease and diabetes. For this reason, they should be eaten only in small quantities.

A balanced diet means eating the right amount of food from each group every day. Together with regular exercise, good nutrition can help people stay healthy and live a longer life.
